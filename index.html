50° is near-optimal for Redmond winters. Winter sun at 18.9° altitude hits a 50° slope at ~69° incidence—much better than a shallow slope. The steep angle also sheds rain and snow aggressively. Grade slopes toward the north wall, draining into the rubble trench foundation.
